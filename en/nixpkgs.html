nixpkgs

nixpkgs (https://github.com/NixOS/nixpkgs) is a monorepo containing a collection of Nix packages. It also includes various utility Nix functions (like writeShellApplication), as well as the NixOS Linux distribution (including the Module System).

In flakes, nixpkgs is the most commonly used input.
